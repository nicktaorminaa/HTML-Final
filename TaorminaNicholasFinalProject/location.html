Location

New York Giants - Location

The New York Giants are based in the New York metropolitan area. They play their home games at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.

MetLife Stadium

MetLife Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium located in East Rutherford, New Jersey. It is the home of the Giants and the New York Jets, who share the stadium in a unique arrangement. The stadium was opened in 2010 and has a seating capacity of 82,500.

Getting to MetLife Stadium

There are several ways to get to MetLife Stadium for Giants games. The stadium is easily accessible by car, with several highways and roads leading to it. There is also a dedicated New Jersey Transit train station near the stadium, which can be reached from New York City and other parts of New Jersey. If you are coming from New York City, you can take the train or bus to the stadium, or you can drive and park in one of the many parking lots near the stadium.
